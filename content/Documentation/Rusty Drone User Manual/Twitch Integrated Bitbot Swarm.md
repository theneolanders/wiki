Rusty Manufacturing's Twitch Integrated Bitbot Swarm is a device that allows chatters to deploy and control Bitbots with limited functionality during a live stream.
# Commands
- !bitbot explode 
	- Explodes your Bitbot
	- If any bits are stored, fireworks will shoot out until the Bitbot is empty, then it will explode
- !bitbot spin [int] 
	- Make your Bitbot spin X times
	- While spinning, a Bitbot has a 50% dodge chance when shot
- !bitbot firework 
	- Spend 20 stored bits to shoot off a firework
# Bitbot Features
- Bitbots move towards a designated target point
- Replying to a fellow chatter causes your Bitbot to face theirs and sets them as your target point 
- Lifespan
	- Base lifespan is 30 seconds.
	- Subscribers have double lifespan
- Using RustyManufacturing emotes or ending your message with a question mark will change the facial expression of your Bitbot
- Bits
	- Bitbots track how many bits have been sent over their lifetime
	- Particles spew out based on the quantity and tier of cheering
- Bitbots can be shot and killed
